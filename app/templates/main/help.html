How to sell a property?

Login as a seller and click 'sell' at navbar to publish a property.

What is the scope of the search for the facilities around the property?

Normally, the range is one kilometer in radius.

Can anyone add a property for sale?

Yes, but after filling in the information and submitting the application, it must be approved by the agent before it can be officially released. The review process generally takes 24 hours.
